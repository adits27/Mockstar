User Personas

Who Uses MockStar

Four primary user archetypes identified through the onboarding schema — each mapped to the platform features that serve them most.

🎓

The New Grad

Recent university graduate · First-time job seeker
Entry level 0–1 years experience High anxiety
Pain Points
  • No professional interview experience to draw on
  • Nervousness causes filler words and gaze avoidance
  • Unsure how to structure answers (STAR method, etc.)
  • Limited real-world examples to reference
Goals
  • Build confidence through repeated, low-stakes practice
  • Get concrete feedback on delivery and structure
  • Learn which filler words to eliminate
MockStar Features They Value Most
Filler word detection Eye contact scoring Clarity & structure dimension Repeat sessions / history
Characteristic Quote
"I want to stop saying 'um' every five seconds and actually sound like I know what I'm doing."
🔄

The Career Switcher

Transitioning to a new industry or function
Mid level 3–8 years experience Domain gap
Pain Points
  • Strong experience but in a different domain — needs to reframe stories
  • Unfamiliar with terminology of the target industry
  • Worried about being penalised for lack of direct experience
Goals
  • Practice translating past experience into relevant narratives
  • Identify and fill industry knowledge gaps
  • Upload job descriptions to get contextually tailored questions
MockStar Features They Value Most
Job description upload Industry fit scoring Experience articulation dimension Resume-grounded feedback
Characteristic Quote
"I've led three product launches — I just need to figure out how to talk about them in data science terms."
🌏

The Non-Native Speaker

International student or immigrant professional
Any level English as second language Accent aware
Pain Points
  • Concerned that accent or slower pace will be penalised
  • May use filler phrases while searching for the right word
  • Cultural differences in interview expectations
  • Needs inclusive, constructive feedback — not judgement
Goals
  • Practice in a safe, non-judgemental environment
  • Get feedback on content quality, not speaking speed
  • Build confidence before high-stakes interviews
MockStar Features They Value Most
Communication challenges flag WPM excluded from scoring Non-penalisation instruction in prompt Answer relevance focus
Characteristic Quote
"My answers are good — I just need to practise without worrying that my accent is hurting my score."
📈

The Senior Professional

Returning to market after a gap or targeting a step-up
Senior level 8+ years experience High standards
Pain Points
  • Hasn't interviewed in years — rusty on format and pacing
  • Tends to over-explain or go off-topic due to breadth of experience
  • Wants to target senior or leadership roles specifically
Goals
  • Sharpen conciseness and structure across multiple mock runs
  • Get feedback calibrated to senior-level expectations
  • Track score improvement over multiple sessions
MockStar Features They Value Most
Clarity & structure scoring Session history dashboard Resume-grounded coaching Behavioural + case study modes
Characteristic Quote
"I know I ramble. I need something that shows me exactly where I lost the thread — and how often."
